DiscVault

Your disc collection, finally organised.

DiscVault is a native iOS app for collectors of physical media. Scan a barcode for instant cover art and metadata, track every edition and region, and see it beautifully — with an optional self-hosted backend so your data stays yours.

Barcode scanning

Scan a disc and DiscVault pulls in cover art and metadata automatically.

Edition tracking

Steelbook, Director's Cut, Limited or standard — record exactly which release you own.

Region logging

Blu-ray A/B/C and DVD 1–6, kept straight per title.

Search & filter

Filter by format, genre or director and find anything in seconds.

Plex & Jellyfin

See which discs you already have digitally — and which you still need to rip.

Backups & offline

Full collection backups, one-tap restore, and offline browsing.

Lending tracker

Always know which disc you lent out, and to whom.

Self-hosted & private

Run the optional Docker backend yourself — your data never leaves your hands.
